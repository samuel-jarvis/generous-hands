What we Do

We Need Your Help

Join the team of dedicated volunteers at Generous Hands Foundation and make a positive impact in the lives of those in need. Your time, skills, and passion can make a real difference, and will help to create a brighter future for individuals and communities around the world.

Volunteering with us is a rewarding and fulfilling experience, and there are many opportunities to get involved, including:

  • Direct Service: Assist with the implementation of our programs and initiatives, and help to provide essential resources and support to those in need.
  • Event Support: Help to plan and execute events and fundraising initiatives, and support our efforts to raise awareness and funds for our mission.
  • Office Support: Contribute your administrative and organizational skills to support the day-to-day operations of our organization.
  • Advocacy: Join our advocacy efforts and help to bring attention to important issues and needs in our communities.
